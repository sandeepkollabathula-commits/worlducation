About Us

Mission: To be the change and facilitate a world without illiteracy by transforming how children learn and increasing school access to technology.

Vision: A world without illiteracy.

Core Values: Ethical principles, Innovation, Collaboration.

Founded in 2016, Worlducation creates tablets and educational content for primary students. Our AI-powered learning adapts to each child’s needs. For every purchased classroom, we donate a classroom elsewhere and connect them to collaborate.

Products & Services

Hardware: Durable, child-friendly tablets optimized for classrooms.

Software & Content: AI-driven personalized learning, curriculum-aligned activities.

Collaborative Learning: Linking classrooms globally for shared learning.

Future Options: Cloud solutions, repurposed tablets, manufacturing expansion.
